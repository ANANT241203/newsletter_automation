Stay Healthy & Connected This Summer!

 

Take time to prioritize your well-being while staying engaged with the Penn community. Enjoy the outdoors, stay active, check in with friends, and explore campus wellness resources available throughout the summer to support your physical and mental health.

 

Additionally, make sure to add and/or update your health insurance information in PennChart to ensure you're covered when you need it.
 

Access Support with Mantra Health

Through Wellness at Penn’s partnership with Mantra Health, students can access teletherapy through the Care Hub, providing virtual one-on-one sessions with culturally informed providers. These providers understand and support the unique mental health needs of college students, and you can discover support wherever you are. Visit wellness.upenn.edu for more information.

 

Stay healthy, stay informed, and take care of your wellbeing this season!”
